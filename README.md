# 职场英语核心语块矩阵（60天完整速成版）

> **老板专属定制**：从 Day 1 到 Day 60 的完整训练计划。每天只需掌握 1 个核心母体句型，通过替换词汇裂变出无数表达。主打“以一当十，脱口而出”！

## 📅 Week 1: 破冰与日常闲聊 (Small Talk & Icebreakers)

### Day 1: 开启话题
* **核心母体**：`How's your... going?`
  * How's your week going?
  * How's your new project going?

### Day 2: 回应与捧哏
* **核心母体**：`That sounds...`
  * That sounds intense.
  * That sounds like a solid plan.

### Day 3: 表达同感
* **核心母体**：`I totally get...`
  * I totally get what you mean.
  * I totally get how frustrating that is.

### Day 4: 委婉推脱
* **核心母体**：`I'd love to, but...`
  * I'd love to, but I have a hard stop at 3 PM.
  * I'd love to, but my plate is full.

### Day 5: 结束对话
* **核心母体**：`I'll let you get back to...`
  * I'll let you get back to work.
  * I'll let you get back to your lunch.

### Day 6: 聊周末计划
* **核心母体**：`Any exciting plans for...`
  * Any exciting plans for the weekend?
  * Any exciting plans for the holidays?

### Day 7: 聊工作进展
* **核心母体**：`How are we doing with...`
  * How are we doing with the Q3 report?
  * How are we doing with the client feedback?

## 📅 Week 2: 会议控场与发言 (Meetings & Discussions)

### Day 8: 表达赞同
* **核心母体**：`I completely agree with...`
  * I completely agree with your point.
  * I completely agree with the new strategy.

### Day 9: 委婉反对
* **核心母体**：`I see your point, but...`
  * I see your point, but we lack the budget.
  * I see your point, but the timing is tight.

### Day 10: 礼貌打断
* **核心母体**：`Sorry to interrupt, but...`
  * Sorry to interrupt, but can I add something?
  * Sorry to interrupt, but we are out of time.

### Day 11: 确认对方意思
* **核心母体**：`So what you're saying is...`
  * So what you're saying is we need more time?
  * So what you're saying is the deal is off?

### Day 12: 提出建议
* **核心母体**：`Why don't we...`
  * Why don't we discuss this offline?
  * Why don't we take a short break?

### Day 13: 救场与争取时间
* **核心母体**：`Let me double-check...`
  * Let me double-check the exact numbers.
  * Let me double-check with my manager.

### Day 14: 会议总结
* **核心母体**：`To wrap up,...`
  * To wrap up, the next steps are clear.
  * To wrap up, let's review the action items.

## 📅 Week 3: 演讲与汇报 (Presentations & Reporting)

### Day 15: 完美开场
* **核心母体**：`Today I'd like to walk you through...`
  * Today I'd like to walk you through the Q3 results.
  * Today I'd like to walk you through our new product.

### Day 16: 过渡与衔接
* **核心母体**：`Moving on to...`
  * Moving on to the next slide.
  * Moving on to the financial overview.

### Day 17: 描述图表数据
* **核心母体**：`As you can see from...`
  * As you can see from this chart.
  * As you can see from the data here.

### Day 18: 强调核心结论
* **核心母体**：`The key takeaway is...`
  * The key takeaway is our growth is accelerating.
  * The key takeaway is we need to cut costs.

### Day 19: 处理Q&A提问
* **核心母体**：`That's a great question. Let me...`
  * That's a great question. Let me explain.
  * That's a great question. Let me get back to you.

### Day 20: 拖延与回旋
* **核心母体**：`I don't have the exact figure, but...`
  * I don't have the exact figure, but it's around 20%.
  * I don't have the exact figure, but I will email you.

### Day 21: 致谢与收尾
* **核心母体**：`Thank you for your time, I'm happy to...`
  * Thank you for your time, I'm happy to take questions.
  * Thank you for your time, I'm happy to discuss further offline.

### Day 33: 强调双赢
